# Assignment 3: Future of AI-Powered Clinical Decision Support Systems

In this assignment, I explored how AI-powered Clinical Decision Support Systems might evolve over the next 5 to 10 years. I discussed where these systems could improve care, such as earlier detection, personalized recommendations, and more efficient clinician workflows.

I approached this as a forward-looking analysis by combining current trends with practical healthcare realities. My conclusion was that AI-CDSS has strong potential, but progress should be balanced with caution around transparency, clinician oversight, and patient safety. I came away with a better understanding of how to weigh innovation against real-world clinical constraints.
