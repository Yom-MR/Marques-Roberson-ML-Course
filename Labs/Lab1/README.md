# Lab 1: AI in Healthcare Glossary

In this lab, I created an alphabetical glossary of key AI terms used in healthcare. For each term, I included a clear definition, a healthcare-specific example, and a short explanation of why the term matters in real clinical or operational settings. My goal was to make technical language easier to understand while still keeping the meaning accurate and useful.

Compiling this glossary helped me better understand how technology, regulation, and ethics are connected in healthcare AI. As I worked through the terms, I saw how concepts like bias, explainability, interoperability, and privacy are not separate issues; they all influence whether an AI system is safe and trustworthy in practice.
