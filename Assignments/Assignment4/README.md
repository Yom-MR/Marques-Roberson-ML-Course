# Assignment 4: Evaluating fast-RSOM Diagnostic Results

In this assignment, I reflected on a diagnostic study involving fast-RSOM and explained key performance concepts, especially sensitivity and specificity. The purpose was to evaluate how well the test identifies true positives and true negatives, and what that means for clinical usefulness.

I approached the assignment by reviewing the reported findings, defining the statistical terms in plain language, and forming my own interpretation of the test's strengths and limitations. I learned that diagnostic performance metrics are essential for understanding reliability, and that responsible evaluation requires looking beyond a single number to overall clinical context.
