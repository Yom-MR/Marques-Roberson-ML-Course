# Assignment 2: Healthcare Datasets

In this assignment, I worked with a small healthcare dataset and calculated the average age and average heart rate. The goal was to practice basic data interpretation and apply simple statistics to health-related values.

I approached the task by identifying the relevant fields, performing the calculations step by step, and interpreting what the resulting averages indicate about the sample. I learned that even simple descriptive statistics can provide useful clinical insight and serve as a foundation for more advanced healthcare analytics.
