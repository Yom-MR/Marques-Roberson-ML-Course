# Assignment 6: Social Determinants of Health

In this assignment, I discussed Social Determinants of Health, including factors like income, living environment, and education. I explained why these factors are critical for understanding patient outcomes and why healthcare decisions should account for more than clinical variables alone.

I approached the topic by connecting social context to health disparities and examining ethical concerns such as privacy, fairness, and potential bias in data-driven systems. My main takeaway was that healthcare AI can only be effective and equitable when social context is considered alongside traditional medical data.
