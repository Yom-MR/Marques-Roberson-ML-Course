# Lab 1: AI in Healthcare Glossary

This lab challenged me to compile an alphabetically organized glossary of fundamental AI concepts relevant to healthcare.  For each term I provided a definition, a real‑world healthcare example and a short explanation of why the concept matters.  The finished glossary covers topics from algorithmic bias and explainable AI to federated learning and telemedicine.

Through this exercise I learned how important it is for multidisciplinary teams to share a common vocabulary when evaluating AI tools.  It also highlighted how technical innovations intersect with regulations and ethics—reinforcing the need for thoughtful governance when adopting AI in clinical settings.

See `lab1.ipynb` for the full glossary.
