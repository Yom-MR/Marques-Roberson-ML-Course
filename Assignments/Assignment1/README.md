# Assignment 1: Medical Terminology

In this assignment, I focused on understanding complex medical terms such as perineal urethrostomy, permanent tracheostomy, macrophages, and related concepts. The main objective was to break down difficult terminology into clear definitions and explanations so I could communicate accurately in healthcare-focused discussions.

I approached this by researching each term, summarizing core definitions, and translating technical language into concise explanations. Through this process, I improved my medical vocabulary and gained more confidence interpreting clinical terms that often appear in patient documentation, medical literature, and healthcare AI contexts.
